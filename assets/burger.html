Breakfast Burger Recipe

If you like breakfast any time of day as well as the flavor of a delicious juicy burger, this recipe is just for you. Granted with the addition of a fried egg and apple butter, this recipe definitely is not for everyone. However, if you're interested in a savory, sweet, overall delicious burger that goes above and beyond restauraunt quality, this might be the burger for you.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (preferably 80/20)
  • Brioche Buns
  • Bacon
  • Cheese (I use havarti, provolone, or American)
  • Burger seasoning of your choice (I combine salt pepper garlic onion smoked paprika brown sugar and chili pepper
  • Fried egg (I definitely prefer runny but yours doesn't have to be)
  • Apple butter (its essentially a fruit spread and has no butter/dairy in it. can find in stores.)
  • Condiments of choice (I always do ketchup KEWPIE mayo pickles lettuce and onion, never tomato for me)

Recipe

  1. Step 1: The first thing you want to do is PREP everything. Make sure your veggies are cut. Measure out your patty size and season it. Preheat your pans. Get your bun ready for everything. This will save you time later on
