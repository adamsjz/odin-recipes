My Recipe Site

Hello everyone, especially those from the Odin Project, to my recipe site! This is a project associated with the Odin Project's introductory course regarding HTML pages, various elements and tags within them, as well as using git to update all the changes.

The Recipes

All the recipes and photos you see are actually of my food! Since food is one of my biggest passions, I thought it would be a great idea to use this project to actually write out some of my own recipes. I thought this would be a nice little personal touch to the project, and that it additonally might give me a fun extra challenge of having to organize all of my own material much like a real website. These are a good variety of dishes that I make regularly, and maybe might be some you wind up making regularly as well!

**Disclaimer**

I have to be fully honest in saying that I do NOT measure anything! I typically make enough to serve 3 but I truly eye ball everything when I cook. This is understandably chaotic for some, but in general if you can sort of figure out measurings that work for you, I guarantee you will love these dishes as much as I (and everyone who has eaten them) do.
